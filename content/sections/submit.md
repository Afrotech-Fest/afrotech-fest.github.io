<h2>SUBMIT A PROPOSAL</h2>

<p>We're casting the net far and wide to find some of the most interesting, diverse and forward-thinking people in our community. We are very keen to hear from techies, makers, digital artists, enthusiasts and community workers whose aims resonate with ours. If you have an idea for a workshop or a talk, a suggestion about who or what else should be included, or even a topic that you feel should be addressed, then get in touch below.</p>

<p>If you'd like to submit a talk, fill out our <a href="https://goo.gl/forms/WXEEJhwJnfK9bdr62">proposal form</a>. For more information on what to submit and what we offer our participants, read our submissions guidelines.</p>

<p>Deadline for submissions is <b>Thursday, 30th of November, 2017</b>.</p>



<p>All festival attendees and participants (speakers, workshop facilitators and volunteers) are encouraged to read our <a href="coc">code of conduct</a> and <a href="accessibility">accessibility</a> information.</p>
